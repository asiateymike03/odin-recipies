Description

Indomie is a quick light delicacy that is enjoyed by people of various age groups. It's original name is ramen and is quite popular among asians and africans. It is categorised as a fast food due to how quick it can be prepared.

Ingredients

  • Indomie Noodles
  • Oil
  • Vegetables
  • Protein
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil Indomie noodles for 3 minutes and drain water.
  2. Pour oil into a frying pan.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry.
  4. Add Indomie noodles and stir-fry.
  5. Add seasoning, chilli and protein.
  6. Stir-fry until contents are mixed properly.

Enjoy your meal.
