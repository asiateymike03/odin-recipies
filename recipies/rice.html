Description

Rice is a carbohydrate cereal that is enjoyed by many around the world. It is particularly enjoyed most by asians and africans. Today's recipe is going to be Fried Rice. It is a very delicious and nutritious dish which goes with a wide range of sauces and proteins.

Ingredients

  • Boiled Rice
  • Oil
  • Seasoning
  • Protein
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chopped Vegetables

Steps

  1. Pour a cosiderate amount of oil.
  2. Add in your vegetables, eggs and stir fry continously.
  3. Add boiled rice and stir-fry.
  4. Add soy suace, seasoning, protein and more vegetables and stir-fry continously.
  5. Stir-fry until even colour is achieved.
  6. Fried rice is ready to be served.

Enjoy your meal.
